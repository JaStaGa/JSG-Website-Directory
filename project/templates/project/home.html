Welcome to MyPlayer Builder

MyPlayer Builder helps you plan out your NBA 2K25 MyPlayer by showing exactly which attributes you’ll need to unlock the badges you want. Pick your height, then select the badges and badge levels you’re aiming for, and we’ll calculate the minimum attribute requirements (plus any dependent boosts) so you can hit your target overall.

In NBA 2K25, your position, height, weight, wingspan, and attributes all factor into which badges and badge levels your MyPlayer can earn. This tool lets you reverse-engineer that process: choose the badges you want, and we’ll tell you the attribute build you need.
