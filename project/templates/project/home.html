NOTE: Every height assumes the preset weight & wingspan

On NBA2K25, players can create their MyPlayer build by selecting their position, height, weight, wingspan, and attributes. These selections play a role in determining the badges and badge levels their MyPlayer can acquire.

This app allows users to plan for their build's creation by selecting the badges they want their player to have. This will show the user what attributes they need to upgrade in order to gain their chosen badges.
